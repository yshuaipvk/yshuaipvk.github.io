Measurement functions

The 4210-CVU can measure these parameter:

  • Parallel capacitance and conduction(Cp-Gp) (并联电容-电导)
  • Parallel capactitance and dissipation factor (Cp-D) (并联电容-损耗因子)
  • Series capacitance and resistance(Cs-Rs) (串联电容-电阻)
  • Series capacitance and dissipation(Cs-D) （串联电容-损耗因子）
  • Resistance and reactance(R-jX) (电阻-电抗)
  • Impandance and phase angle(Z-theta) (阻抗-相位角)

The time domain of the AC current and AC voltage must be processed into the frequency domain to
produce the phasor form of the DUT impedance.
The capacitive impedance (and conductance) are calculated based on the measured AC impedance
and phase.

The capacitance is calculated from the capacitive impedance and the test frequency using the
formula:

CDUT = Capacitance of the DUT(F)
IDUT = Current of the DUT
f = Test frequency(Hz)
VAC=Measured AC voltage

Test signal

  • 1 kHz to 10 kHz in 1 kHz increments
  • 10 kHz to 100 kHz in 10 kHz increments
  • 100 kHz to 1 MHz in 100 kHz increments
  • 1 MHz to 10 MHz in 1 MHz increments

The AC signal output level can be set from 10 mVRMS to 100 mVRMS (1 mVRMS resolution). The output impedance is 100 Ω (typical). The AC voltage measure range is 100 mVRMS.The ranges available to measure current are 1 μA, 30 μA, or 1 mA. With autorange selected, range selection is done automatically.

C-V Sweep for solar cells

C‑V measurements can be made either forward-biased or reverse-biased. However, when the cell is forward-biased,the applied DC voltage must be limited; otherwise, the conductance may get too high for the capacitance meter to measure. The maximum DC current cannot be greater than 10mA; otherwise, the instrument’s DC voltage source will go into compliance and the DC voltage output will not be at the desired level.
